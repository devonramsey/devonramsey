Devon Ramsey Bio

My professors have aptly prepared me for an internship in research and analysis.

As a high school student, I privately tutored students in Algebra, Statistics and Calculus. I also have office experience; during the summer of 2014 I worked at a local medical practice where I answered phones, greeted patients and handled routine office tasks.
